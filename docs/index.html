Overview

A single video shot on a smartphone is enough. No specialty hardware, expensive scanners, or specialist software required. Upload the photos and video you captured—SFACSTUDIO reconstructs the site in 3D, true to the real place.

SFACSTUDIO is a digital twin editor for industrial facilities, built on 3D Gaussian Splatting. From full factory lines down to single assets—reconstruct and extract freely by space, equipment, or object. Edit on the reconstructed space, change layouts, run simulations—all in one browser window.

No site visit required. No design drawings required. Just capture—and your digital twin begins.

Why SFACSTUDIO

Why the product matters—and why you can trust it.

CAD · point cloud · panorama

Limits of conventional approaches

Hand-built 3D modeling is costly, slow, and often unrealistic. Photogrammetry lacks detail and needs heavy manual cleanup. Panoramic video is not true 3D—you cannot interact with other models in the scene.

3DGS & proven tech

Proven 3DGS engine

Based on research that won an Outstanding Paper Award at APIC-IST 2025: foreground and background are learned separately and combined with adaptive 3D fusion for temporally consistent, photoreal rendering.

Simple workflow

Three steps are enough

Site capture → 3D generation → editor rendering. Reconstruct in 3D right away without specialists or deep technical skill—from indoor and outdoor spaces down to individual equipment.

Digital twin features

Spatial reconstruction, redesign, simulation, and information visualization—organized in one overview.

Space reconstruction

Full-space reconstruction, equipment restoration, and object-level extraction—low cost and a practical base for many digital twin workflows.

Space redesign

Remove existing equipment and place new objects. Separate, move, delete, or replace assets by unit to review equipment-change scenarios on the real-site twin.

Simulation

Path-based motion on 3D objects. Simulate AMR/AGV routes and logistics flow on the real-space twin.

Information visualization

Memo pins, data links, and pop-up details on equipment. Manage inspection history in space; highlight risk zones and anomalies with color for quick identification.
